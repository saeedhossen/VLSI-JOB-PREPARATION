ABOUT VLSI INDUSTRY

Very Large Scale Integration (VLSI) is a solid career choice and offers job opportunities for EEE freshers pursuing core employment. In Bangladesh and overseas, VLSI provides a variety of employment roles featuring outstanding professional growth and salary incentives. It's all about the design of integrated circuits which is usually referred to as a Chip design. The demand for VLSI-qualified professionals is increasing as the design and production industries increase.

VLSI Career Opportunities In Bangladesh:

Six companies are already actively running chip design service businesses in Bangladesh.Neural Semiconductor Limited, Ulkasemi Limited, and PrimeSilicon Limited are the three firms that offer standard chip design services, while sBit Inc., Linear Micro & Next Silicon Ltd. are the three that offer EDA tools and assistance to help others.Two new big players ACI and Walton are also set to enter the market and Synapse Design already entered the market.
At present we have about 400 to 500 VLSI engineers in our country. But the sector is growing very fast which requires more self-motivated engineers who actually want to build a career in this promising field.
And the average starting salary is 30,000-45,000 BDT per month which varies from company to company and on the performance of employees.
